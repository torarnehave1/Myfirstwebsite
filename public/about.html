About SlowYou

Welcome to SlowYou

SlowYou is a transformative platform promoting holistic well-being through bioenergetic exercises integrated with principles of mindfulness and body awareness. We provide a collaborative, open-source environment where individuals and organizations can adapt and extend our methodologies to create personalized wellness programs.

Our Philosophy

At SlowYou, we believe in the power of community and shared innovation. Our open-source philosophy enables participants to create derivative works, like SlowYou Dance or SlowYou Yoga, tailored to specific needs and experiences. This approach not only enriches our offerings but also fosters a vibrant community of practice that supports continuous learning and development.
